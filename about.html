About No Rings Attached

Welcome to No Rings Attached, India's first matchmaking platform exclusively for DINK (Dual Income, No Kids) enthusiasts. We understand that choosing to live a child-free life is a significant personal decision, and finding a partner who shares this vision can be challenging.

Our platform was born from a simple observation: there are many individuals who have consciously chosen the DINK lifestyle but struggle to find like-minded partners in traditional matchmaking spaces. We created No Rings Attached to bridge this gap.

What sets us apart is our focus on creating meaningful connections between people who share the same life goals and values. Our community is built on mutual respect, understanding, and the celebration of personal choices.

We believe that everyone deserves to find a partner who understands and respects their life choices. Whether you're focused on career growth, personal development, or simply enjoying life's adventures without the responsibilities of parenthood, we're here to help you find your perfect match.
