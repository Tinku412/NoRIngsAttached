Important Subscription Updates

Creating a Safe Community Experience

July 20, 2025 3 min read

TL;DR - Quick Summary

There is now a fee to access complete profile details. All existing users continue to have free access till September 1, 2025. New users from today (July 20, 2025), have a basic fee to be able to access complete profile details. Check out pricing details below.

When this site was first launched, it was not in my mind to make people pay for it.

I wanted to keep it free and open to the community. Any costs incurred would be covered by myself, and I was going to keep it that way.

This was just my way to give back to the community, and also to have something to keep me busy during the weekends. And believe me, as a child-free, single, introvert, nerd – I don't have anything better to do on a weekend!

But, practicality differs from expectations.

Over the past few days, I have removed multiple fake profiles & uninterested users who are just testing the site.

Based on the feedback and response so far, I've realized that having a free for all access is not good for the below reasons:

  1. Privacy Concerns: Users want to remain separated from the outside world due to stigma around being child-free.
  2. Safety Issues: Few users have been hesitant to upload their profile pictures or add more personal details to their profiles due to lack of a safe space.
  3. Non-serious Users: There were few non-serious users just scrolling for fun.
  4. Resource Waste: Limited resources getting wasted from fake profiles and spam users.

To maintain the quality of profiles with only high intentional users, below are the fee details that will be implemented:

What does this mean for you?

Existing Users: If you are an existing user, you will have no impact & will continue to have all access till September 1, 2025. Post September 1, the fee will be applicable to you, depending on the feedback and reassessment.

New Users: Currently, the fee will be applicable only for new users who sign up from today, that is July 20, 2025.
